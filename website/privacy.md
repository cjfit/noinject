# Ward Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 3, 2025

## What We Collect

**Your Google Account Email** - Only if you choose to sign in using Chrome Identity API.

**Why we need it:**
- Create and manage your account
- Sync settings across devices
- Provide customer support
- Enable premium features
- Send service updates

## What We DON'T Collect

**All threat scanning happens locally on your device. We do NOT collect:**
- Websites you visit
- Content you scan
- Any browsing data

Everything stays on your device.

## Your Control

- Sign-in is optional - Ward works fully without an account
- Revoke access anytime through Google Account settings
- Request data deletion: support@tryward.app
- We never sell your email

## Contact

Questions? Email support@tryward.app
